In recent years Formula 1 has faced and continues to face harsh criticisms that the races are far too predictable. The team decided to put this criticism to the test but building multiple models and comparing the results to the official 2019 race results.

TWO models were used because the SVM model did a better job predicting who cames first while the Random forest models does a better job of predicting which bin a given racer falls into. Bins are position groups which racers fall into with bin 1 being racers that finish in the top 3, bin 2 being racers 4-6, bin 3 being racers 7-10 and bin 4 being racers 10 onwards.
